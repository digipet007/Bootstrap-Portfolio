About Me

I seek a challenging and impactful role as a web developer in the Seattle area. I love creating robust technologies using Javascript, jQuery, React, Node.js, responsive web design, Express.js, and MySQL. I recently completed my certificate from the University of Washington in Full Stack Web Development. After six years as an educator, I realized my love for coding and problem-solving while completing my Master’s in Education from Dominican University.

My Master’s program focused on topics in educational technology, such as game design, infrastructure, coding, microcontrollers, and best practices in leveraging technologies to provide access to the curriculum. My background in public education has fostered a skillset in project management, communication, and collaborating with a diverse team of professionals. I highly value working with a team that has a similar passion for growth and education.

When I am not programming or teaching, I can be found hiking, painting, reading, and playing Dungeons and Dragons.
